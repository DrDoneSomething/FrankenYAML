Nested dictionaries are used to create sub-entities. A good use for this is a settings integration with a dictionary{configure_dict_integrations} you wish to break out into individual files or objects.

Example: homeassistant/customize
Entities location: customizations/entities
[integrations/homeassistant.yaml]
homeassistant:
  customize: !include_dir_merge_named ../customizations/entities/

[customizations/entities/binary_sensor.bob_home.yaml]
binary_sensor.bob_home:
  icon: mdi:human-male
